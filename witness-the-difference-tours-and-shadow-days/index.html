Shadow Days

Have you been curious about Lumen Christi Catholic School and what being a Titan would be like?

The best way to find out is to actually be one for a day. Following participation in the Informational Tour on any of the dates indicated with an asterisk (Shadow Days), prospective students have the opportunity to participate in our Titan for a Day program to see what being a Lumen Christi Titan is all about. Each prospective student is matched with a current Lumen Christi student, whom he or she will shadow for the day. Learn firsthand about our school, programs, and our curriculum. Attend classes and enjoy an LC lunch. Meet and ask questions of current students, faculty, and staff. Find out for yourself what makes Lumen Christi special!

Come and spend the day with us! There is no obligation or cost to attend this event. To RSVP or if you have questions, please email Maureen Brown at mbrown@myjacs.org
