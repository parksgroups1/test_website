Technology

To view these auras with all the features, please do the following:

  • download the Aurasma app
  • open it
  • click on “A” at the bottom of the screen
  • the search “magnifying glass” at the bottom of the screen
  • click on “Channels” at the top of the screen and in the search field enter “sjschool”
  • click on follow
  • This will give you access to all of our Aurasma project via our channel.
  • Click on the “picture frame” at the bottom of the device screen, once the bouncing dots begin, hold your device in front of the picture.
